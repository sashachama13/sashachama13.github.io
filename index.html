About Me

I believe there is no limit to creativity. Pushing ideas and concepts as far as the human imagination can take you is what it’s all about. I never pigeonhole myself to committing to one style. My approach is versatile to suit individual needs. I stay on top of all current and rising design trends. Don’t let that fool you into thinking I like to use gimmicks. I am all about meticulous, purposeful, concept driven design with a twist.

Why I Create

Solving problems by exhausting every possibility, through the creative process to achieve visual harmony is my passion. This is also something that I enjoy immensely, have a lot of fun doing and am pretty good at. Very much like shifting around puzzle pieces until everything falls into place. Theres nothing more exciting than being able to translate someones idea, vision, or message visually to an audience however large or small to create a lasting impact.

BRANDING

Doi Chaang Coffee Co.

A Logo re-design, for a fair trade coffee company based in Vancouver. Their logo is a detailed illustration of the Doi Chaang tribe leader’s face. Which pays homage to the people from their village and Thailand as a whole. The illustration however meaningful, does not work as a logo.

The Process

To represent the Doi Chaang Coffee company I chose to combine two strong identifiable images into one dynamic recognizable shape. I wanted to unify their heritage with their business in order to continue to honor their people.

The Process

Cirque du Soleil Annual Report

The objective was to create an annual report for Cirque du Soleil out of a raw text document. The challange was to two create two versions in English and French. Which had be layed out identically in both languages to manintain a unified look throughout the report.

The Process

Grabbing the readers attention with a striking visual, enticing them to read on further. The design solution for the copy, was to pair it with dynamic photographs that encapsulated the essence of what Cirque du Soleil is about.

INTERACTIVE

Healthy Heart App Design

The purpose was to create an app that performs a singular function well. It is a iOS app that helps the user calculate nutritional information such as calories, cholesterol, sodium and sugar when dining out at a restaurant. HealthyHeart is intended for people who are suffering form heart disease or may be at risk of heart disease, but may also be used by anyone as it is intended for healthy living.

The Process

Through researched material I was able to create a smooth, simple, and easy to use interface.
