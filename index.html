About Me

I believe there is no limit to creativity, pushing ideas and concepts as far as the human immagination can take you is what it’s all about. I never pidgeonhole myself to commiting to one style. My approach is versatile to suit individual needs. I stay on top of all current and rising design trends, don’t let that fool you into thinking I like to use gimmicks. I am all about meticulous purposeful concept driven design with a twist.

Why I Create

Solving problems by exhausting every possibility through the creative process and achieving visual harmony is my passion. This is also something that I enjoy immensely and have a lot of fun doing. Very much like shifting around puzzle pieces until everything falls into place. Theres nothing more exciting than being able to translate someones idea, vision, or message visually to an audience however large or small, creating a lasting impact.

Branding

Doi Chaang Coffee Co.

Logo re-design, for a fair trade coffee company in Vancouver. The original logo is a detailed drawing of the Doi Chaang tribe leader’s face which pays homage to the people in the village and Thailand as a whole. The illustration however meaningful, does not work as a logo.

The Process

To represent the Doi Chaang Coffee company I chose to mix two strong identifiable images into one dynamic recognizable shape. I wanted to combine the Thai heritage of the Doi Chaang with their business in order to continue to honor the people

Articulous Renovations

A logo redesign for an existing Ottawa based renovations company.

The Process

Taking the most recognizable elements of renovations, and fuzeing them together with the company’s name . In the process amining to portray a quality, proffessional and reliable business image.
